Programme

State of the Map 2019 is taking place from 21 to 23 September 2019 in Heidelberg, Germany. We have created an exciting program with youâ€”the OpenStreetMap community. For the duration of the conference, we have rooms set aside for breakout sessions that can be booked by groups of mutual interest, or used without prior planning.

  • Saturday, 21 September: conference begins with an opening session, followed by a day of presentations and workshops
  • Sunday, 22 September: day 2 of more presentations and workshops plus a whole track of academic talks selected by our scientific committee
  • Monday, 23 September: the final day continues more talks and workshops

If you want to use an app to get reminder for talks in the schedule, you can use Giggity. Alternatively, you can get the whole schedule as calendar file.
